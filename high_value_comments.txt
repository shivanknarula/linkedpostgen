I gave a robot eyes, and taught it to make decisions. 👁️🤖
For my ME6 robotics project, I integrated YOLOv8 real-time object detection into a ROS2-powered robot simulation. The result? A robot that sees the world through a camera, understands what's in front of it, and reacts autonomously.
Final output: the exact location of detected object in 3D is published to "detections" topic and based on what is it and the location of it close or far? the decision node decides what will happen (stop, slow down, go left, ...) and publish it to the ME6 Robot on "/cmd_vel" topic.
Tech stack:
ROS2 Humble | Python | YOLOv8 (Ultralytics) | OpenCV | Gazebo | cv_bridge | geometry_msgs
This project gave me hands on experience at the intersection of AI and robotics, a space I'm deeply passionate about and actively building in.
