🚀 How do we teach ROS 2 differently with Odyssey?
Most ROS 2 learning relies on documentation, exercises, or tutorials. With Odyssey, we take a different path: we turn ROS 2 into an interactive experience.
The image below captures the core ideas behind our approach:
🔍 Unveil the mechanisms of ROS 2
 Players discover how topics, nodes, and tools behave by interacting with them inside the game world. Concepts are visual, spatial, and hands-on.
🤖 Explore worlds with Odie
 Learning begins through exploration. Each environment introduces ROS 2 ideas in context, supported by narrative and in-game systems. The desire to discover more thrive the player in learning.
🧩 Solve puzzles using real ROS 2 tools
 Players use the actual ROS 2 CLI and code to solve challenges.
📈 Advance toward increasingly complex tasks
 From basic pub/sub interactions to managing packages, nodes, and diagnostics in multi-robot scenarios, each chapter builds on the previous one.
🛰 Learn to manage robotic fleets
 Later levels introduce multi-robot coordination, and system-scale reasoning, reflecting real robotics challenges.
🛠 From game to reality
 All ROS 2 commands and code used in Odyssey follow standard practices. Skills acquired in-game are directly applicable to physical robots and real projects. Odyssey is not simulating ROS2, but directly interfaced with it.
